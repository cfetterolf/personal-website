Fours iOS App October 2016 - Present
FounderMiddlebury, VT

In my free time at school I created Fours, a free KenKen puzzle generator available on the App Store. The game allows you to play as many puzzles as you want, generating over 600,000 possible board combinations.

I began the development process knowing nothing about iOS development, and the path from concept to published product taught me invaluable lessons about both software design and myself. I began the project by researching and inventing my own method of generating unique puzzle boards, then implemented my algorithm in Swift as the core component of the app. I then built additonal features to help the app become more than just a puzzle generator - features such as challenge modes, global leaderboards, and a step-by-step tutorial that helps teach newcomers how to play.

Throughout this process I've learned that nothing motivates me more than a captivating project. Even though I created Fours simply as a way to play more KenKen puzzles, I hope it can now become a platform to share my passion for KenKens with others.
